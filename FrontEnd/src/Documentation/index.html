Introduction

Star Travel is a Premium HTML5 Responsive Multipurpose Template specially designed for Travel, Tour, Flight, Hotel, Car and Cruise booking/listing related business. Its features give your business a great boost and covert your visitors into real time customers.

Star Travel Template included 50+ HTML Pages with 6 different home pages, headers and footers. It's come with 8 attractive colors which give you more choice to show.

HTML Structure
